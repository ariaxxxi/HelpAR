PREP

Add ingredients & liquid into your glass jar

Do not fill the jar above 80% of its volume to avoid pressure accumulating.

Place the head onto the glass jar and twist until tightly sealed.

Check for leakage before placing it on the motor base.

Turn the jar upside down, twist and lock it onto the base.

Always grab the jar by the handles to avoid possible spilling

Turn on the mixer by rotating the knob.

Mixing should not require longer than one minute to yield smooth and creamy results.

Untwist the jar using the handles.

Do not remove the head until blending has completely ceased.

Flip the jar towards its initial position and untwist the head. Enjoy!

Always point contents away from your body when unsealing
