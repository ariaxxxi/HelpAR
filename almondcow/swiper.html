Fill

Fill the base with water to the MIN or MAX line (or in between).

Add

Add dry ingredients to the filter basket up to the MAX / 1 cup line.

Twist

Secure the filter basket to the top of the Almond Cow by twisting the basket to the right.

Place

Place the top & power cord to the base. A solid blue light indicates it is ready to use.

Press

Press the cow button! A white flashing light indicates that milking is in process. When the blue light is solid, your milk is ready.

Enjoy

Remove the top of the Almond Cow and stand it in the collector cup. Pour your milk into a container and store in the fridge. Enjoy for 3-5 days.
